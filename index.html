Welcome to the World of Little Woody

Step into a heartfelt adventure with Little Woody, a 2D puzzle-story game that blends hand-crafted visuals, brain-teasing puzzles, and an emotionally captivating journey.

Explore. Solve. Remember.

Guide Little Woody, the last of his kind, through a mysterious world filled with curious machines and forgotten memories. With every puzzle you solve, a piece of his past is revealed.
